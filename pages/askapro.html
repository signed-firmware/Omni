Ask A Pro

Main Page Ask A Pro

Stuck on a build, dont know if your staging is cooked, or just need someone who actually knows what they're talking about? This is where you go.

How it works
  1. Join the Neo Shredder Group Discord.
  2. Find the help channel (or whatever channel the mods have set up for questions).
  3. Explain what you're trying to do, what you've already tried, and post screenshots or a short clip if you can.
  4. Wait for someone who isn't an omni main to respond.
Before you ask
  • Check the wiki first — massless, gyro cores, and armor info cover most beginner problems.
  • Search the discord if the question's been asked before.
  • Don't ask us to review an omni build. We will judge you.
Tip: The more specific your question, the faster you get a useful answer.
Warning: Nobody wants to debug your build blind. Show your modules.
